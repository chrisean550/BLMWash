Petitions

Name Description
George's Law A petition to create “George’s Law,” a law that would make racially motivated crime an act of terror and make any race/hate groups a terrorist organization.
PA School Curriculums Demand that Pennsylvania schools live up to their academic standards and include more Black History in school curriculums.
HANDS UP ACT The Hands Up Act is a proposed piece of legislation that suggests officers receive a mandatory 15-year prison sentence for the killing of unarmed men and women.
#WEAREDONEDYING The NAACP launched the petition in honor of George Floyd with the sole purpose of eliminating senseless hate crimes.
Reallocate Assets Join the Black Lives Matter movement, which aims to redirect funds to invest in Black communities.
STAND WITH BREONNA This one’s dedicated to Breonna Taylor, who was murdered when police wrongfully entered her Kentucky apartment.
JUSTICE FOR AHMAUD ARBERY In honor of Ahmaud Arbery, who was killed while jogging—unarmed—in Georgia.
JUSTICE FOR BELLY MUJINGA Belly Mujinga (a railway worker from London) died from COVID-19 after she was denied proper protection as an essential worker.
JUSTICE FOR TONY MCDADE The petition seeks justice for Tony McDade, a transgender man who was killed by police in Tallahassee.

Donations

Name Description
Official BLM Movement Black Lives Matter Foundation is a global organization in the US, UK, and Canada whose mission is to eradicate white supremacy and build local power to intervene in violence inflicted on black communities by the state and vigilantes.
Reclaim the Block Reclaim the Block is an organization founded in the city of Minneapolis that aims to bring the community and city council members together to build policies that will move funds from the police department to other areas of the city’s budget that are in dire need of more support.
The Bail Project The Bail Project™ National Revolving Bail Fund is on a mission to combat mass incarceration and reshape the pretrial system in the United States.
R.E.S.I.S.T. This campaign is centered on providing the resources, healing, tools and build a place where these words can thrive and live.
Black & Brown Founders They provide community, education, and access to Black and Latinx entrepreneurs, allowing them to launch and build tech businesses with modest resources.
D.R.E.A.M D.R.E.A.M. is a nonprofit organization committed to empowering underrepresented, urban youth by equipping them with the essential knowledge for life’s challenging financial decisions.
Pretty Brown Girl Is an organization dedicated to educating and empowering girls of color by encouraging self-acceptance while cultivating social, emotional & intellectual well-being.
Color of Change Dedicated to challenged injustice and advanced solutions for racial justice that can transform our world.
Black Voters Matter Primary focus is to increase voter registration and turnout as a way to have a greater impact on policies.
Third Wave Fund Goal is to ensure young women, queer, and trans youth of color have the tools and resources they need to lead powerful movements, and that they have a seat at the table within philanthropy.
