Inspire

Minority Mentorship

Building Relationships:

With our INSPIRE mentorship program, mentees can expect to develop long-lasting relationships and create a network of support with their mentors and other black and brown students.

Providing Guidance:

INSPIRE will also be a resource for minority students to improve their professional skills and gain insights into the next stage of life. Mentees can expect to develop strategies for coping with personal and academic challenges.

Empowering Youth

Our mentors will inspire our members from underrepresented groups to make smart decisions in working towards their goals and to become the leaders of their generation.
